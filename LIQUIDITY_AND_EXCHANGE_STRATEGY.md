## üí° Liquidity Pool Mechanics

### **How DEX Liquidity Works**

**Example: Raydium MSHW/USDC Pool**

**Setup**:
- Provide $50,000 liquidity
- Split: $25,000 MSHW + $25,000 USDC
- Creates MSHW/USDC trading pair
- Users can swap MSHW ‚Üî USDC

**How It Works**:
1. User wants to buy MSHW
2. Swaps USDC ‚Üí MSHW
3. Pool receives USDC, gives MSHW
4. Price adjusts (more USDC, less MSHW = price up)
5. **Automated Market Maker (AMM)**

**Liquidity Provider Benefits**:
- Earn trading fees (0.3% per trade)
- Price appreciation (if MSHW goes up)
- **Risk**: Impermanent loss (if prices diverge)

---

### **Liquidity Mining Incentives**

**How It Works**:
1. Users provide liquidity (MSHW/USDC)
2. Receive LP tokens (liquidity provider tokens)
3. Stake LP tokens
4. Earn MSHW rewards

**Example**:
- Provide $10,000 liquidity
- Receive LP tokens
- Stake LP tokens
- Earn 100 MSHW per week
- **Incentivizes liquidity provision**

**Cost**: Development fund allocates MSHW for rewards
