1. **Accurate Description**: To accurately describe or refer to Mondoshawan Protocol

3. **News/Media**: For news reporting, commentary, or criticism

### You May NOT Use Our Trademarks:

1. **Domain Names**: Using "mondoshawan" or "mshw" in domain names

2. **Product Names**: Using our trademarks as your product name

   - ❌ "MSHW Exchange" (unless authorized)

3. **Misleading Use**: Suggesting endorsement or affiliation
   - ❌ "Official Mondoshawan Partner" (unless true)
   - ❌ "Mondoshawan Team" (unless authorized)

4. **Confusing Similarity**: Using similar names that cause confusion
   - ❌ "Mondoshawan2"

## Guidelines for Use

### When Using Our Trademarks:

1. **Use as Adjective**: Use as an adjective, not a noun
   - ✅ "Mondoshawan blockchain"
   - ❌ "the Mondoshawan" (use "the Mondoshawan blockchain")

2. **Proper Attribution**: Include proper attribution
   - ✅ "Mondoshawan is a trademark of Mondoshawan Protocol"

3. **No Modification**: Don't modify or combine with other marks
   - ❌ "MondoshawanCoin"
   - ❌ "MSHW Token" (if referring to a different token)

4. **Distinctive Styling**: Use distinctive styling when possible
   - ✅ Use official logo when available
   - ✅ Use proper capitalization: "Mondoshawan" not "mondoshawan"
